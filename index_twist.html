    About Me

    I'm Cantonese Singaporean, born and raised in California, interested in understanding human behavior through data and technology in order to improve the mental, physical, or financial well-being of others. As a former consultant and public accountant, I have an appreciation of business strategy, data, and bottom-line results. When I'm not working, I enjoy eating at new restaurants, reading, learning something new, yoga, meditation, or sipping a cup of tea.

    Here's a snippet of my ongoing journey in technology:

    • 2014-Present

      Master's Candidate, UC Berkeley School of Information

      Coursework in user experience research, design, prototyping, and entrepreneurship. Goals to learn ballet, scuba, and singing before graduating.

    • May-July 2013

      Co-Founder at The Founder Institute

      Selected to join a 12-week, part-time entrepreneurship program where I co-founded SuiteSplit, a marketplace for sharing hotel rooms and democratizing hotel bookings.

    • 2009-2014

      Consultant by Day, Student by Night

      While a former consultant at AlixPartners doing restructuring, fraud investigations, and business development, I learned teamwork, analysis, and project managmement. In Fall 2012, I completed Stanford online courses in Human-Computer Interaction and Technology Entrepreneurship. Prior to that, I was an assurance associate at a big-four accounting firm, where I earned my CPA license.

    • December 2008

      Earned Bachelor's degrees in Psychology and Business Administration from UC Berkeley

      Graduated one semester early from UC Berkeley with a double major. With the extra time, I studied abroad in Beijing where I took immersive Business Chinese and had one of the best times of my life.

    • 2007-2008

      Co-Founded Simlio

      Although this site is no longer live, this was my first foray into entrepreneurship. We co-founded Simlio in order to make the stock market more accessible to the public through simulated portfolios, back-testing, and customized trading algorithms.
